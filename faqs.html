🐾 Dogs

Yes — dogs are genuinely welcome at Hazel Edge. We accept up to two dogs per stay, and there is no additional charge. Dog towels and a mat are provided for muddy arrivals, and the garden is fully enclosed.

Yes. The garden is fully enclosed and safe for dogs to enjoy off-lead. Beyond the garden boundary, the wider land is a working smallholding — please keep dogs on leads if you venture beyond the garden.

Yes, plenty. There are footpaths close to the property, and within a short drive you have Talkin Tarn Country Park (dog-friendly with a café), sections of Hadrian's Wall Path, and the wider Solway and North Pennines areas. We include a local walking guide in your welcome information.

We ask that dogs are kept off the furniture and beds, please. This helps us keep the cottage fresh for all guests, including those who stay after you. Dog beds are welcome — please bring your own.

No. Dogs are welcome at no additional cost. We do ask that you clean up after them in the garden and on any parts of the wider land you access.

🏡 The property

Hazel Edge has an outdoor bathing area for guests to enjoy. Please check the listing or get in touch for current details on what is available during your dates.

Hazel Edge sleeps up to 6 guests across three bedrooms. The bedrooms can be configured as doubles or twins depending on your group's needs.

Yes. Hazel Edge is a good family home, and children are very welcome. The enclosed garden is safe for younger children to play in. Some parts of the outdoor terrain are uneven, so younger children will need supervision in the wider areas.

There is private off-road parking at Hazel Edge for up to two vehicles, as well as EV charging. Full arrival instructions are sent in advance of your stay.

💧 Water & utilities

The water at Hazel Edge comes from a private spring on the hill — fresh, clean and tested. It is one of the quiet pleasures of staying here. Because it is not a mains supply, we ask guests to use water thoughtfully and avoid leaving taps running unnecessarily.

Hazel Edge is on a private septic tank. Please flush only toilet paper — nothing else, including wipes (even those labelled "flushable"). Bags and a bin are provided in the bathroom. This is an important one — thank you.

Hazel Edge is heated by an air source heat pump — efficient, quiet and reliable. It warms the cottage quickly and maintains a comfortable temperature throughout the year. Electricity is supplied via Octopus on a renewable tariff.

Yes. There is EV charging available on site. Please get in touch before your stay if you have specific requirements about charging speed or connector type.

📋 Practical info

Yes — fibre broadband is available throughout the cottage and works well. WiFi details are provided on arrival. Mobile signal varies by network in this area, but WiFi calling works reliably on most phones.

Carlisle is approximately 15 minutes by car and has a full range of shops, supermarkets, independent cafés and restaurants. Brampton, a smaller market town, is also about 15 minutes away and has a good butcher and local shops. Rural, but genuinely not remote.

Check-in is from 4 pm and check-out is by 10 am. If you need a different arrangement, please get in touch and we will do our best to help — it depends on the bookings either side of your stay.

We live nearby at Liddel Lodge, so we are close if you need anything — but Hazel Edge is your space to enjoy in peace. We will not be dropping in uninvited. You will have our contact details and we are always available if something comes up.

♿ Accessibility

As a single-storey bungalow, Hazel Edge has no stairs — all rooms are on one level. The wet room has step-free access with a level threshold. Some outdoor areas have uneven ground. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us directly and we will be as helpful and honest as we can about what will and will not work for you.

Yes — a detailed access guide is available on request, covering door widths, floor surfaces, bathroom dimensions, parking distance and outdoor terrain. Please get in touch and we will send it to you.

📅 Booking

The minimum stay is two nights for most of the year, and three nights during peak periods (school holidays and bank holidays). Longer stays are very welcome — the cottage feels even better after a few days.

You can book direct via this website for the best rate — no platform commission, and you deal with us personally. Hazel Edge is also available on Airbnb and Booking.com. All calendars are kept in sync, so availability is always accurate wherever you book.

Our direct booking cancellation terms are included in your booking confirmation. If you are booking via Airbnb or Booking.com, their standard cancellation policies apply. We try to be reasonable where we can — if circumstances change, please talk to us.

For direct bookings, a deposit secures your dates with the balance due before arrival. Please contact us if you would like to discuss the payment schedule — we are happy to be flexible where we can.
