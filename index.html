Welcome to Hazel Edge

Nestled in the ancient woodland of Nicholforest on the Scottish border, Hazel Edge is a beautifully restored traditional bungalow offering a true escape from the everyday. Surrounded by wildlife-rich land, ancient oaks, and the sounds of the River Liddel, this is a place to breathe deeply and reconnect with the natural world.

Sleeping up to 6 guests, with 3 bedrooms and ample outdoor space, Hazel Edge is perfect for families, friends, and those seeking a genuine countryside retreat. Dogs are warmly welcome.
