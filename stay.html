A warm and comfortable base

Hazel Edge is a three-bedroom bungalow set in private grounds on the edge of our smallholding in northern Cumbria. It sits above a wide open valley with views across the border country — the kind of landscape that makes you stop and look.

Inside, it is warm, well-equipped and designed for easy living. You have everything you need for a relaxed stay, with the feel of a home rather than a rental.

Outside, there is a private enclosed garden, an outdoor bathing area, and the wider countryside beyond. Rural, but not remote — Carlisle is fifteen minutes away.

Practical details

Good to know

We believe in being straightforward. These are the things worth knowing before you arrive.

💧 Spring water

Hazel Edge is supplied by a private spring on the hill — fresh, clean and natural. Because it is not a mains supply, we ask guests to use water thoughtfully. Not anxiously, just with the awareness that it is something precious.

🚽 Septic tank

Hazel Edge is on a septic tank. Please flush only toilet paper — nothing else. Bags and a bin are provided. This is a simple but important way to help us care for the land and water around us.

📍 Rural location

Hazel Edge is a working smallholding setting. It is quiet, private and genuinely rural — there will be birdsong, weather and the sounds of the countryside. The nearest shops are fifteen minutes away in Carlisle.

📶 WiFi & signal

Fibre broadband is available and works well. Mobile signal varies by network — we recommend checking yours in advance, though WiFi calling works reliably throughout.

♿ Accessibility

As a bungalow, Hazel Edge has no stairs. The wet room has step-free access. Some outdoor areas have uneven ground. Please contact us if you have specific requirements — we will be as helpful and honest as we can.

🌡️ Heating

Hazel Edge is heated by an efficient air source heat pump. The cottage warms up quickly and stays comfortable throughout the year. The wood burner in the living room is there for atmosphere as much as warmth.

Four legs welcome

Bring the dog

Dogs are genuinely welcome at Hazel Edge — not as an afterthought, but as part of the welcome. The garden is enclosed, the setting is rural, and the countryside around is made for dogs who like to roam.

We accept up to two dogs per stay. There is no extra charge, but we do ask that they are kept off the furniture and beds. Dog towels and a mat are provided for muddy arrivals.

Nearby walks start from the door, and longer routes — including sections of Hadrian's Wall and the Eden Valley paths — are within easy reach.

Dog-friendly details
  • Private enclosed garden — safe off-lead space
  • Dog towels and mat provided
  • Dogs welcome in all rooms except on furniture and beds
  • Please clean up in the garden and on the land
  • Keep dogs on leads on the wider smallholding
  • Maximum 2 dogs per stay
